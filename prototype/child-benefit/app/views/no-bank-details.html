No - UK bank account or building society

Child Benefit must be paid into a bank account, building society account or Credit Union, this can be in somebody else’s name, but you are responsible for making sure you get the money. Child Benefit can only get paid into one account

You can get the money paid into any account, apart from:

  • a Nationwide cashbuilder account (sort code 07 00 30) in someone else’s name

Please provide bank account or building society details for the Child Benefit claim as we cannot back date more than 3 months.

Contact the Child Benefit office if you need additional support:

  • Online: Webchat link
  • Telephone: 0300 200 3100
  • Outside UK: +44 161 210 3086
  • Our phone line opening hours are: Monday to Friday: 8am to 6pm. Closed weekends and bank holidays.
