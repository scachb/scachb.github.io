Income details

If you or your partner have an individual income of:

  • below £50,000 - There is no tax charge if your and your partner's individual incomes are less than £50,100 in a year.
  • between £50,000 and £60,000 a year - a tax charge of less than the Child Benefit payment will apply, so you may want to be paid Child Benefit
  • more than £60,000 a year - a tax charge equal to the Child Benefit payment will apply, so you may not want to be paid Child Benefit
! Warning You or your partner's income is is between £50,100 and £60,000 over £60,000 in a year. The person with the highest income may have to pay a tax charge, called the High Income Child Benefit Charge. You must report any change of circumstances to the Child Benefit Office (opens in new tab). If you are overpaid Child Benefit you might have to pay it back.
